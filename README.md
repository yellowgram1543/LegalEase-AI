# 📜 App Description – *LegalEase AI*

**LegalEase AI** is your intelligent assistant for understanding legal documents.
Contracts, rental agreements, loan terms, or service policies often hide crucial details in complex jargon — making it difficult for everyday people to fully understand what they’re signing.

Our app simplifies this process by providing **clear, actionable insights** while ensuring privacy and ease of use. With a simple upload, LegalEase AI helps you uncover risks, obligations, loopholes, and more — empowering you to make informed decisions.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 Single-Document Analysis

* **Summary** – A clear, concise overview of the document.
* **Pros & Cons (Role-specific)** – Tailored advantages and disadvantages based on your role (e.g., *Tenant*, *Landlord*, *Borrower*).
* **Loopholes Detector** – Identifies ambiguous or exploitable clauses.
* **Risk & Obligation Finder** – Flags red-alert clauses, highlights your duties, and lists questions you should ask a lawyer.
* **Clause & Jargon Explainer** – Paste any confusing clause to get a simple explanation, term definitions, and real-world examples.
* **Document Q\&A** – Ask natural questions about your document and get context-based answers.
* **Scenario Simulator** – Explore “What if…” situations (e.g., *What if I miss a payment?*) to see possible outcomes.

### ⚖️ Comparative Analysis Mode

* **Side-by-Side Comparison** – Upload two documents to view differences in terms, risks, and obligations.
* **Visual Risk & Cost Analysis** – Compare obligations, penalties, and costs with easy-to-read tables and charts.
